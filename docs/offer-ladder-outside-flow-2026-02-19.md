# AgentReach Offer Ladder Outside Flow (2026-02-19)

## Goal
Create productized services that can be sold with or without software adoption, then upsell into Flow.

## Offer Stack (Recommended)

### 1) Database Reactivation Sprint (Done-for-you)
Who: agents with old CRM and low follow-up.

Includes:
- CRM cleanup and segmentation
- 21-day email/SMS reactivation campaign
- booking link setup
- weekly reporting

Price:
- `$1,500-$3,000` one-time setup
- optional `$50-$150` per booked call

Why it sells:
- fast "cash this month" outcome
- clear ROI story

### 2) Digital Mayor Launch Kit
Who: agents with no consistent local content engine.

Includes:
- niche positioning and topic map
- 30-day local content calendar
- 12 short-form scripts + 4 newsletter drafts
- publishing SOP

Price:
- `$1,000-$2,500` setup
- `$500-$1,500/month` ongoing

### 3) Hyper-Local Channel Accelerator
Who: teams specializing in one neighborhood/sub-market.

Includes:
- "Living in [Submarket]" channel setup
- 90-day content architecture
- thumbnail/title packaging and SEO metadata
- monthly performance reviews

Price:
- `$2,500-$5,000` setup
- `$1,000-$2,500/month`

### 4) Owned Audience Growth System
Who: agents dependent on rented leads.

Includes:
- lead magnet production (city/vibe/relocation guide)
- landing page + form + auto-responder
- weekly newsletter operating cadence
- referral prompt scripts

Price:
- `$1,500-$4,000` setup
- `$497-$1,497/month`

### 5) Story-Driven Listing Media Package
Who: listing agents needing differentiated marketing.

Includes:
- property story framework
- short-form narrative assets
- listing journey updates (inspection/negotiation/progress)
- "community impact" content angle

Price:
- `$500-$1,500` per listing pack

### 6) Business Alliance Flywheel Program
Who: relationship-first agents in tight local communities.

Includes:
- local business interview pipeline
- partner spotlight content templates
- co-marketing outreach scripts
- referral loop tracker

Price:
- `$750-$2,000` setup
- `$300-$1,000/month`

## Packaging Strategy

Entry offer:
- Database Reactivation Sprint (fast ROI)

Core recurring:
- Lead Revival Engine + Owned Audience Growth

Authority upsell:
- Digital Mayor Launch Kit + Hyper-Local Accelerator

Enterprise/team upsell:
- Alliance Flywheel + custom media ops retainers

## Suggested Positioning
- "We help real estate teams operate like local media companies and convert old contacts into new closings."
- "Flow is the system, our services are the execution arm."

## Sales Script Angles
1. Replace lead-rental dependence.
2. Monetize the database already paid for.
3. Build owned audience assets compounding month-over-month.

## Fulfillment Model

Team pods:
- Account strategist (client comms + KPI)
- Content operator (scripts + newsletter)
- Automation operator (sequence + QA)
- Optional appointment setter

Capacity model:
- 1 pod handles 12-20 standard accounts depending on service mix.

## KPI Targets by Offer

Database Reactivation Sprint:
- reply rate > 8%
- booked call rate > 2% of enrolled leads

Digital Mayor Kit:
- 4+ local content posts/week
- 1 newsletter/week

Owned Audience System:
- list growth 3-8% monthly
- open rate benchmark against client baseline (+20% relative target)

## What to Sell First (Next 60 Days)
1. Lead Revival Engine beta (5-10 clients).
2. Database Reactivation Sprint as done-for-you service.
3. Digital Mayor Launch Kit as upsell for best responders.

## Qualification Filters
- Minimum CRM size: 1,000+ contacts.
- Existing email domain and compliant sender setup.
- Willingness to publish at least 2 pieces/week.

## Standard Terms
- No guarantees on closings.
- Clear definition of "booked call" for performance pricing.
- Client owns content and audience data.
