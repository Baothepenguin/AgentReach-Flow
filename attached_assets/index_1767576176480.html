Happy December!

The holiday season is in full swing!

It’s one of my favorite times of the year because the energy across Dallas is just magical, and there's a real sense of community warmth despite the cooler weather.

As we wrap up 2025, I want to sincerely thank you for your trust and support this year, it means the world to me. Whether you're cozying up at home or out enjoying the lights, I hope you take time to relax and celebrate with the people you cherish most.

Please know I’m here and ready to talk real estate or mortgages when the festivities wind down and you start planning for the New Year!

Victoria Antonyk

Broker & REALTOR®
Fun Local Events this Week!
December 1 – January 5 | The Trains at NorthPark This beloved holiday tradition features 750 feet of track with intricate miniature scenes benefitting the Ronald McDonald House of Dallas.
NorthPark Center, 8687 N Central Expy, Dallas, TX 75225

December 1 – January 4 | Dallas Zoo Lights Enjoy a beautiful walk-through experience with over a million twinkling lights, larger-than-life animal lanterns, and a visit to Santa's Workshop.
Dallas Zoo, 650 S R L Thornton Fwy, Dallas, TX 75203

December 1 – January 4 | ICE! at Gaylord Texan & Lone Star Christmas Walk through a nine-degree winter wonderland featuring two million pounds of hand-carved ice sculptures and thrilling ice slides.
Gaylord Texan Resort & Convention Center, 1501 Gaylord Trail, Grapevine, TX 76051

December 1 – January 1 | Vitruvian Lights A spectacular display of over a million sparkling LED lights wrapped around the trees in Vitruvian Park, making for a perfect evening stroll.
Vitruvian Park, 3966 Vitruvian Way, Addison, TX 75001

December 1 – December 24 | SNOWDAY and Santaland at Galleria Dallas An immersive, explorative adventure through imaginative rooms at the North Pole and a chance to meet Santa Claus.
Galleria Dallas, 13350 Dallas Pkwy, Dallas, TX 75240

December 1 – December 28 | Christmas Movies at the Palace The historic Palace Theatre screens classic Christmas movies on various days and times throughout the month for family enjoyment.
Palace Theatre, 300 S Main St, Grapevine, TX 76051

December 5 | Christmas on the Square A festive evening in Garland featuring a tree lighting, fireworks, snow, visits with Santa, and holiday performances.
Downtown Square, 520 W State St, Garland, TX 75040
Are you coming to any of these? Let me know!
